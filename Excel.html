Excel Projects

This project delves into the sales performance of Adidas during the years 2021 and 2022, examining regional sales, insights from retailers, and the distribution channels used, including outlet, in-store, and online sales. Through a detailed analysis, this dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Adidas' sales landscape, offering comparisons with competitors.

Key Findings:
Sales Trend: In 2020, sales experienced a surge in April, reaching a peak value of USD 24,607,006, followed by a subsequent decline. The lowest sales month was observed in December, with a value of USD 8,026,527.

Sales Method: In 2020, 51% of purchases were made in-store, followed by 45% in outlets, and online purchases accounted for 24% of total sales.

Retailer Insights: In 2020, the retailer with the highest sales was West Gear, while Foot Locker emerged as the top retailer in 2022.

Recommendation: Based on the analysis provided, it is recommended that Adidas focuses on optimizing its online sales channel, as it represents a significant portion of the overall sales and has the potential for further growth. Additionally, understanding the factors contributing to the fluctuation in sales throughout the year, especially during peak months like April, can help in developing targeted marketing and sales strategies to capitalize on high-demand periods. Furthermore, building strong partnerships with retailers like Foot Locker, who have demonstrated success in selling Adidas products, can enhance brand visibility and sales performance.
